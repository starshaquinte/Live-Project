LAND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

Theatre Vertigo humbly acknowledges we lie and work on the unceded, traditional tribal lands

of the Multnomah, Wasco, Clackamas, Chinook bands, Kathlamet, Tualatin Kalapuya, Cowlitz, Mollala

Cowlitz, Mollala, Cascades, Oregon City Tumwater, Confederated Tribes of the Grande Ronde and other indigenous peoples

We are on stolen land

Theatre Vertigo has been and continues to examine our community relationships to ensure our

collaborators, partners and sponsors support the our regions Indigenous Nations and communities.

One of the ways Theatre Vertigo is working to act on our commitment to being of service to the lands

that we now share is to build brdges between our arts community and local Nations
